---
title: PreviewKeyDownHelper
---

Simple helper for controling text inputs.

# Methods
|Name|
|-|
|RejectLetters|
|AcceptLettersOnly|
|AcceptNumbersOnly|
|AcceptNumbersAndOperators|

# Example

```xml
 <NumberBox PreviewKeyDown="{x:Bind dev:PreviewKeyDownHelper.AcceptNumbersOnly}"/>
```
