---
title: DragMoveAndResizeHelper
---

Drag your window by set a UIElement as a Drag Region

# SetDragMove

```cs
DragMoveAndResizeHelper.SetDragMove(window, element);
```

# UnsetDragMove

```cs
DragMoveAndResizeHelper.UnsetDragMove(element);
```
