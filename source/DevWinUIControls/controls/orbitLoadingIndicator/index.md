---
title: OrbitLoadingIndicator
---

A composition-based loading indicator with orbiting animations to show progress.
# Property

|Name|
|-|
|IsBusy|
|IndicatorColor|

# Example

```xml
<dev:OrbitLoadingIndicator IsBusy="true"/>
```
