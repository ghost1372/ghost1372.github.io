---
title: FlipCards
---

A container of multiple FlipBlocks to show time, date, or multi-digit values with dynamic flipping animations.

# Attributes
|Property|
|-|
|Value|
|Source|

# Example

```xml
<dev:FlipCards Source="Time" />
```
