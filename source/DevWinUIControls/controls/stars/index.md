Animated sky background with stars

# Property

|Name|
|-|
|StarUri|
|SkyVisualAreaRatio|
|NumberOfSmallTwinklingStars|
|NumberOfBigMovingStars|

# Example

```xml
<dev:Stars Height="300" />
```
